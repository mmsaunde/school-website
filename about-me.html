Get To Know Me

An Introduction

I'm Marissa Saunders, a junior at Syracuse University pursuing a degree in Sports Management with a minor in Public Communications. I have earned a place on the Dean's List every semester of my freshman and sophomore years. In addition to excelling academically, I compete as a Division I Track and Field hurdler—an experience that has taught me resilience, time management, and how to thrive in high-pressure situations.

Experience

My journey in sports has already provided me with a wide range of experiences across operations, recruiting, and sponsorships. I currently serve as an On-Campus Recruiting and Operations Student Assistant with Syracuse Football, where I support the daily recruiting operations of a Power 4 program.

Previously, I interned with Penn State Football and the Columbus Fury of the Pro Volleyball Federation. At Penn State, I was an integral member of the events operations staff, planning official visits and travel for more than 50 recruits. With the Fury, I created storyboards to help secure new corporate partnerships and developed strategies to increase community engagement.

Community Service

Beyond athletics and internships, I am actively involved in leadership and service on the Syracuse campus. As a member of the Diversity & Inclusion Student-Athlete Board (DISAB), I contribute to initiatives that enhance the student-athlete experience. Additionally, I serve on the Falk Dean's Student Leadership Council, collaborating with peers and administrators to enrich the overall student experience.

Long-Term Goal

My ultimate goal is to combine my passion for sports, communication, and relationship-building to pursue a career in college athletics—perhaps as an athletic director at a major university. Whether in the classroom, on the track, or through professional opportunities, I strive to bring passion, dedication, and a growth mindset to everything I do.
